Mwl. Julius K. Nyerere

A Hero from Tanzania

Here's a timeline of Mwl. Nyerere's life:

  • 1922 - Born in Butiama, Mara
  • 1949 - Awarded scholarship to Edinburgh for his Masters of Arts in Economics and History
  • 1952 - Awarded Masters of Arts in Economics and History, goes back to Tanganyika
  • 1954 - Successfully founded TANU party after uniting different political factions in the country and continues the struggle for independence
  • 1961 - Achieves national independence of Tanganyika, becomes first Prime minister
  • 1962 - Becomes first President of Republic of Tanganyika
  • 1964 - Pioneers the union of The Republic of Tanganyika and The Revolutionary Government of Zanzibar under Sheikh A. Karume to form The United Republic of Tanzania (URT), Becomes the first President of URT
  • 1976 - Nehru Award for International Understanding
  • 1982 - Third World Prize
